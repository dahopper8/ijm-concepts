How it works
01
Your group sets a goal
Commit to a 12-month collective giving target. IJM assigns a named region and outcome matched to your commitment level.
02
Meet your field contact
An actual IJM field team member — not a donor rep — sends your group quarterly updates and joins a call once a year.
03
Share within your community
IJM provides a story package and sharing toolkit. Your community distributes it. The work spreads through your network, not ours.
04
Close the year together
Year-end impact summary tied to your specific region. Then decide — renew the commitment, or let someone else in your network take the next cohort.
