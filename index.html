International Justice Mission · Strategic thinking

Big hands.
Bigger voice.

IJM does extraordinary work. Child trafficking in Kolkata is down 86%, independently verified. Trafficking in Cebu dropped 79%. Nine of nine Justice System Strengthening Programs reduced violence by 50–85%. Most people — including most donors — have no idea. These concepts explore what it looks like for the org's voice to match what its hands have accomplished.

The core tension
IJM has the most credible proof record in the human rights sector — peer-reviewed, third-party audited, longitudinal. That proof infrastructure is almost entirely absent from how IJM communicates, fundraises, and builds donor relationships. Every concept here starts from that gap.
Everything here is rough and lo-fi — built to spark a conversation, not ship to production.

Justice Delivered. Justice Underway.
The strip above is a single visual idea that scales across every surface IJM touches — site pages, email headers, social banners, display ads. The concept is the same everywhere: show what's been won in blue, show what's in motion in amber, dissolve forward to "Until All Are Free." IJM has a proof record most donors don't know exists. This makes it visible, ambient, and shareable — without an ask attached. Below: the full interactive version and five animated ad formats.
